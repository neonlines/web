Overview

A Techno Arcade Race

Use the Wheel to control your line and try to avoid crashing against the walls or crashing against other lines.

Every second you survive you earn points, but also every other Line that crashes makes you earning points.


Avoid your enemies or make them crash agains your line.


Compatible with Game Center.




Privacy

Neon Lines does NOT track your activity in any way, not Analytics nor any other event or behaviour metrics. Whatever you do with this app is up to you and we don't want to know about it.
